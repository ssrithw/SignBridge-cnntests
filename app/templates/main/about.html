About SignBridge

Bridging communication through technology

Who We Are

We are a team of final year Computer Science students committed to developing technology that focuses on bridging communication gaps between Deaf and hearing individuals through real-time sign language translation.

Our Mission

To develop a browser-based prototype that enables real-time Sri Lankan Sign Language fingerspelling recognition.

Our Vision

A future where language barriers between hearing and deaf individuals are eliminated through intuitive, AI-powered tools that anyone can use from a web browser.
